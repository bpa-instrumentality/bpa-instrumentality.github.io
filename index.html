The Violin

The violin is a classical string instrument which consists of 4 strings: G, D, A, and E. The violin is a great instrument to learn playing, and helps build a strong foundation in music. Violins range from about $15 to 50$ a month, depending on the violin's size and quality. They come in a range of sizes, but the most common are the 1/4, 1/2, 3/4 and full size violins.

Fun Fact: Violin bows are generally made of 150-200 hairs.
